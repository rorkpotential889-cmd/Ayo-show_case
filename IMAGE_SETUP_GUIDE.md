# Image Setup Guide

## Where to Place Your Images

### Mobile App Images

Place your mobile app screenshots/images in:

```
public/projects/urban-anomaly-app.jpg
```

**Supported formats:** `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`

### ComfyUI Workflow Images

Place your ComfyUI workflow screenshots in:

```
public/workflows/comfyui-portrait-workflow.jpg  (Photorealistic Portrait)
public/workflows/comfyui-anime-inpaint.jpg     (Anime Character Inpainting)
public/workflows/comfyui-animatediff.jpg        (AnimateDiff Video Generation)
```

**Supported formats:** `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`

## How to Add Images

1. **Take screenshots** of your workflows/apps
2. **Save them** with the exact filenames listed above
3. **Place them** in the corresponding `public/` directories
4. **Refresh** your browser - images will appear automatically!

## Image Recommendations

- **Resolution:** At least 1920x1080 for best quality
- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for screenshots with text
- **File Size:** Keep under 2MB for fast loading
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 4:3 works best
